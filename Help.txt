Welcome to the Help Menu 

To add a new cabinet, please click "Add cabinet" and select the dimension and color of the cabinet. Please fill up every gap.

To delete a cabinet, please select an existing cabinet in your order, and click "delete".

To edit a cabinet, please select an existing cabinet first, then click "edit".

To finalise an order, please click "Finished".

Enter your customer details to sign up/in as a Kitbox client. Confirm your order, wait for your order ticket to be printed and then continue to cashier.

Please feel free to contact anyone of our storkeepers for any other information pertaining your order.
